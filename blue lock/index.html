Blue Lock

About Blue Lock

Blue Lock is a popular anime and manga series created by Muneyuki Kaneshiro (author) and Yusuke Nomura (illustrator).
It revolves around the world of soccer (football) and focuses on a unique and high-stakes competition designed to find the best striker in Japan.

Plot Summary

The story of Blue Lock takes place after Japan's national soccer team faces a humiliating loss at the World Cup.
In an effort to revamp Japan's soccer future, the Japanese Football Union launches a project called Blue Lock.
This project aims to create the world's best striker by gathering 300 young, talented forwards from across Japan.

Blue Lock's Unique Approach to Soccer

The Blue Lock program forces players to focus on individual talent and the desire to win at any cost.
Traditional team dynamics are put aside in favor of personal growth and pushing players to break their limits.
In contrast to typical sports teams where teamwork is emphasized, Blue Lock's philosophy encourages a highly competitive, almost cutthroat mentality.


Egoists

Yoichi Isagi,Meguru Bachira

Yoichi Isagi

Role: Protagonist
Bio: A forward with a keen sense of awareness and adaptability. He initially struggles with confidence but grows into a key player throughout the series.
Goal: To become the world’s greatest striker.

Meguru Bachira

Role: A highly skilled and unpredictable forward.
Bio: Known for his bizarre, chaotic playing style, he relies on his instincts and creativity on the field.
Goal: To discover his "true self" through soccer.
